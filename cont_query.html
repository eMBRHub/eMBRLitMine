eMBRLitMine: Literature mining for bioremediation

Welcome to the eMBR literature mining tool.
This tool will improve identification of bacterial species that could be used for bioremediation of specified contaminants.
It is based on co-occurrence of contaminant and bacterial species in published literature*, resulting in a network of bacteria species found to be associated with a chosen contaminant.
